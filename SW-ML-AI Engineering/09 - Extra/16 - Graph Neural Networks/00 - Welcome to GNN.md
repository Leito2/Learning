# 🕸️ Welcome to Graph Neural Networks

Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) extend deep learning beyond grids (images) and sequences (text) to graph-structured data — molecules, social networks, recommendation systems, and knowledge graphs. Where CNNs see pixels in a grid and Transformers see tokens in a sequence, GNNs see nodes connected by edges with arbitrary topology. This course covers the three foundational architectures (GCN, GAT, GraphSAGE) and their production applications.
