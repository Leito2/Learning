# 🌉 Welcome to Transversal Skills

## What will you learn?

This vault covers the essential skills that bridge [[Software Engineering]] and [[Machine Learning]] / [[AI Engineering]]. While algorithms and frameworks get the spotlight, the abilities to communicate, lead, and operate in English often determine long-term success.

You will learn how to:

- Read and write technical English with confidence, from research papers to RFCs
- Communicate complex ideas clearly to any audience, using storytelling and data
- Navigate the engineering career ladder and grow into technical leadership roles

## Why Transversal Skills Matter

In the tech industry, hard skills are the baseline. What differentiates a good engineer from a great one is the ability to articulate ideas, collaborate across cultures, and influence decisions. These transversal skills are not "soft" — they are force multipliers that amplify the impact of every line of code you write.

Whether you are preparing for your first job or aiming for a staff-plus role, mastering these skills will accelerate your career trajectory.
